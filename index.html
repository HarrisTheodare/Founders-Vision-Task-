Our Guiding Philosophies

 
 
 

Founder Friendly

As we are founders ourselves - we understand founders burning desire to realize their vision and the many great challenges they face in their startup journey. We help them climb their mountains.

 

Purposeful Profit

We actively look for and back early-phase startup companies that focus on making the world a better place by solving major world problems through profitable, scalable and sustainable solutions.

Impact Investment

We focus our time and investment on making lasting positive change on the world by backing companies that aim to make the right impact on our planet through their innovations.

 

Strategic Support

We partner up strategically with companies through financial banking, expertise and guidance, and strategy planning to turn them into innovative, impactful, and lasting businesses.
